Work Samples

Application 1

Landscaping Client Database and Accounting System

Built with Ruby on Rails
I was inspired to build this application while watching my father, a landscaping-business owner, struggling with his record keeping process.

The application manages client information and has functionality for accounting, generating invoice PDFs, and work estimates. His business currently uses my application to manage their invoicing and accounting.

The version you see here is a work-in-progress version 2.

Application 2

LinkBoy

Built with Ruby on Rails
My friends and I share a Slack workspace and we're always sending eachother links and they eventually get lost in the ether. I built this so we could have an easy way to keep track of our links.

LinkBoy is a Slack App that adds a bot user to your workspace. Simply invite LinkBoy to a channel you want to track links in. You can see the links back on the website.

Application 3

Five Year Journal

Built with Ruby on Rails
Daily journaling is a proven way to better help us deal with what life throws at us and also help us remember the good times and the things we can be grateful for.

Five year journals give you a snap shot of your life, one day at a time. Each page in a five year journal is dedicated to a single day, subdivided into 5 sections, so as time passes you can look back and see what things were like.

Application 4

Ear Trainer

Written in Ruby
This is one of the first Ruby programs I wrote! As a musician and aspiring audio engineer, I built this program to help myself, and others like me, hone our ears. The program has several functions: Training to learn frequencies of different tones, training to discern the difference between lossy and losslessly encoded music, and tests to check your progress.
